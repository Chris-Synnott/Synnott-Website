About Me

Hello! Welcome to my personal developer website! My name is Chris Synnott and I am currently a senior Computer Science student at University of California, San Diego. Programming and solving problems has always been a passion for me. Here’s a quick story of how I became interesting in computing:

“When I was young I remembered seeing Facebook or some other giant tech website go down and then hearing the corresponding support account say, “Sorry for the inconvenience (Blah...blah)... our software engineers are investigating the issue.” This really struck me as a 13 year old. With a site so large as Facebook with millions of users and data, how could they ever find the issue so quickly? It just seemed nearly impossible, but I wanted to learn the secret. Over time, while studying computer science and learning, I realized that the process of debugging and breaking problems into smaller ones is something I enjoy.

Anyways, I am always open to new opportunities to solve problems or even the little things such as feedback on this website. Send a message to me below and/or connect with me on Linkedin! Thanks for visiting!
